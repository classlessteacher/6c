What is parkinsons

Parkinsons is a disability that the eldery get once they obtain a certain age. It cuases one limb to shake, this will mostly occor in the wrist joint. This will make it hard for these poeple to do basic daily activities.

Our Product

The P.A.B is an arm brace which allows people with parkinsons to stop shaking........

How we got the idea

As we were brain stroming for ideas we came up with the problem that people with parkinsons don't have a device which keeps them from shaking. So we had to fix that with the P.A.B.
